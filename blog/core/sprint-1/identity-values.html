Identity, Values & Strengths

Using the evidence gathered, describe your core personal values.

Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.
Culture plays a huge role in our lives, so much so that it has been called "the way of life for an entire society." It shapes the way we think, see the world, our values and our identity. The choices we make need to align with our values and how we feel we fit into the world our place” as such. My values are centred around people particularly those closest to me and how I can help make their lives better without expectations. Honesty is a another that has been influenced by my culture, whether that is in what we say, we do, even how we interact with others in an authentic manner.

Using the evidence gathered, describe your strengths and limitations.
My strength lies in a constant yearning and determination to learn and grow, never remaining stagnant and constantly developing both on a personal and professional level. My limitation can sometimes be a lack of confidence in my abilities.

Evaluate your strengths and limitations in terms of your learning and career development.

Identify which of your strengths might help you in your learning journey and how they intersect with learning obstacles.
My keen desire to constantly learn will propel me forward and push me to work past my self doubt in my abilities on my learning journey.
